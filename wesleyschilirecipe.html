Soybean Chili Recipe


Image source same as link at bottom

The Ingredients (check off ingredients as needed):

Shared Ingredients:
Soybeans - 1 Cup
Rice - 2 Cups
Green Onion - 1 large onion, chopped

Ingredients Unique to this Dish:
Garlic - 2 cloves
Oil - 1 tbsp
Tomato - 2 tomatoes, sliced
Tamato Paste - 3 tbsp
Paprika - 2 tsps
Sugar - 1 tbsp

The Recipe:

  1. Using a sacucepan, heat the oil over medium heat while browning the garlic and green onions (1 to 2 minutes)
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and stir
  3. Simmer for 1 hour, stirring and adding seasoning as needed
  4. Add the chili over rice
